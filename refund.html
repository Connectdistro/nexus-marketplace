Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refunds.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Overview

NEXUS aims to ensure every transaction is a positive experience. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued for purchases made on our platform. All payments are processed securely by Stripe.

1. Digital Art & Downloads

Digital files (illustrations, photographs, generative art, 3D models, etc.) are eligible for a refund only if:

  • The file was not delivered within 24 hours of purchase confirmation
  • The delivered file is corrupted, unreadable, or materially different from the listing description
  • The seller misrepresented the license type or usage rights

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase. Once a digital file has been successfully downloaded, it is non-refundable unless one of the above conditions applies.

2. Advertising Services

For advertising and marketing service packages:

  • Full refund: If service has not yet commenced and cancelled more than 48 hours before the scheduled start date
  • Partial refund: If service has partially commenced, a pro-rated refund for the uncompleted portion may be issued at our discretion
  • No refund: For completed services, or if cancellation occurs less than 24 hours before service start
  • Performance guarantees (e.g., impressions, clicks) are non-refundable once campaign has run, unless the seller materially failed to deliver agreed deliverables

3. Entertainment & Subscriptions

  • Single-purchase content (music, films, show tickets): Refundable within 24 hours of purchase if content has not been accessed or downloaded
  • Subscription plans: No refunds for the current billing period; cancellation takes effect at end of current period
  • Live event tickets: Refundable up to 72 hours before the event. Non-refundable within 72 hours unless the event is cancelled or postponed by the organizer

4. Physical Goods (if applicable)

For physical art prints, merchandise, or physical media:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Item must be in original, unaltered condition
  • Buyer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect
  • Refund is processed within 5–10 business days of receiving the return

5. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund:

  • Contact us via our Contact Form or email support@nexusmarketplace.com
  • Include your order number, the item purchased, and the reason for your refund request
  • Attach any supporting documentation (screenshots of errors, proof of non-delivery, etc.)
  • We will respond within 2 business days

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe. Processing times:

  • Credit / debit card: 5–10 business days
  • Bank transfers: 3–5 business days
  • NEXUS platform credits are refunded immediately

7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us, your account may be suspended pending investigation. We cooperate with all payment processor dispute processes.
