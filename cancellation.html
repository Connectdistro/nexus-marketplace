Cancellation Policy

How to cancel orders, services, and subscriptions on NEXUS.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Order Cancellations — Digital Products

Digital product orders (art files, music, video, downloads) cannot be cancelled once the file has been made available for download. If you have not yet downloaded or accessed the file, contact us within 1 hour of purchase and we will attempt to cancel the order.

2. Order Cancellations — Services

For advertising services, creative commissions, and marketing campaigns:

  • More than 48 hours before service start: Full cancellation and refund
  • 24–48 hours before service start: 50% cancellation fee applies
  • Less than 24 hours before service start: No cancellation; full charge applies
  • After service has begun: Only the uncompleted portion may be eligible for a pro-rated refund at seller's discretion

3. Subscription Cancellations

NEXUS Pro and premium subscription plans may be cancelled at any time:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • You retain access to paid features until the period ends
  • No partial refunds are issued for unused subscription days
  • To cancel, go to Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription
  • Alternatively, email billing@nexusmarketplace.com

4. Event Ticket Cancellations

  • More than 72 hours before event: Full refund to original payment method
  • 48–72 hours before event: 75% refund
  • Less than 48 hours before event: Non-refundable
  • Event cancelled by organizer: Full refund issued automatically within 5 business days
  • Event postponed: You may choose to transfer your ticket to the new date or receive a full refund

5. Cancellations by Sellers

If a seller cancels an order or fails to deliver a service, buyers will receive a full refund. Sellers who repeatedly cancel accepted orders may have their accounts suspended or permanently removed from the platform.

6. Account Cancellation

To permanently close your NEXUS account:

  • Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account
  • Or contact support@nexusmarketplace.com
  • Outstanding balances must be settled before deletion
  • Active seller listings will be removed immediately
  • Sellers with pending payouts will receive final payment within 14 business days
  • Purchase history and receipts are retained for legal compliance for 7 years

7. How to Cancel

To cancel an order or service:

  • Visit your Order History and select the order
  • Click "Request Cancellation" if available
  • Or contact us immediately via our Contact Form or support@nexusmarketplace.com
  • Include your order number and reason for cancellation

8. Processing Time

Approved cancellations and refunds are processed within 2–5 business days. Funds are returned to the original payment method per our Refund Policy.
