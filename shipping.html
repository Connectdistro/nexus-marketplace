Shipping Policy

Delivery information for physical goods available on NEXUS.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Digital Delivery (Primary)

The vast majority of NEXUS listings are digital products — art files, music, video, advertising services, and entertainment content. Digital items are delivered electronically and are available for immediate download or access after payment is confirmed. No physical shipping applies to digital purchases.

Physical Goods

Some creators on NEXUS offer physical products such as limited-edition art prints, canvas reproductions, merchandise, physical media (vinyl, DVD), or ceramics and sculptures. Shipping policies for physical goods are set individually by each seller and will be displayed clearly on the listing page before purchase.

Standard Shipping Timeframes

  • United States (domestic): 5–10 business days standard; 2–3 business days expedited
  • Canada & Mexico: 7–14 business days
  • Europe: 10–21 business days
  • Rest of World: 14–30 business days

These are estimates provided by sellers. NEXUS does not control shipping carriers or timelines.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are set by individual sellers and displayed at checkout before purchase confirmation. Costs vary based on item size, weight, destination, and shipping method selected.

Customs, Duties & Taxes

For international shipments, customs duties, import taxes, and fees may apply upon entry into the destination country. These charges are the sole responsibility of the buyer and are not included in the purchase price or shipping fee. We recommend checking your local customs regulations before purchasing physical goods from international sellers.

Tracking

When available, sellers will provide tracking information via email after the item ships. Tracking availability depends on the shipping carrier and method selected.

Lost or Damaged Items

If your physical item arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please:

  • Contact the seller directly through your NEXUS order dashboard within 5 business days of expected delivery
  • Photograph any damage for your records
  • If the seller cannot resolve the issue, escalate to NEXUS support via our Contact Form

Claims for lost or damaged items are handled per our Refund Policy.

Address Accuracy

You are responsible for providing accurate shipping address information at checkout. NEXUS and sellers are not liable for non-delivery due to incorrect address information provided by the buyer.

Contact

For shipping questions: support@nexusmarketplace.com or our Contact Form.
